# Design References & Inspiration

Curated library of all design references, animations, and components for the SecondBrain Cloud build.

---

## 🎨 Brand Direction

**Dark Theme (Primary):** Apple Silicon aesthetic
- Deep metallic black base
- Brushed silver/graphite gradients
- Fine glass borders
- Crisp SF Pro-like typography
- Subtle iridescent accents
- "Command center" / "lab instrument" feel

**Light Theme (Secondary):** Y Combinator aesthetic
- Cream/paper-like off-white background (#fdfaf5-ish)
- YC orange accents (#ff6600)
- Minimal, editorial, confident
- Inter / serif pairing
